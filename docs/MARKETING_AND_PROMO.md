# FantasyChama Master Marketing & Promo Strategy

## Target Market
1. **Core Audience:** FPL Managers in Kenya/East Africa.
2. **Key Demographics:** 18-35 year olds, heavily engaged in football banter, WhatsApp groups, and sports betting.

## Acquisition Pillars

### 1. The "Chairman's Club" (B2B2C)
The most efficient way to acquire 20 players is to acquire 1 Chairman.
- **Pitch:** "Stop fighting with Excel sheets and chasing M-Pesa payments. FantasyChama automates your mini-league."
- **Incentive:** Chairmen automatically receive a 4% cut of the gross pot as an "Admin Fee". If their league wagers 10k per week, the Chairman makes passive income just for moving their WhatsApp group to the app.

### 2. Instagram & TikTok "Banter" Virality
FPL culture is inherently toxic and banter-heavy. Use this to drive engagement.
- **Content Pillar 1 - "The Excel Admin":** POV skits of a stressed admin manually calculating FPL ties and fighting over unpaid 50 bob stakes.
- **Content Pillar 2 - "The Relegation Threat":** Memes about the guy in the red zone who has to pay 50 KES up-front.
- **Content Pillar 3 - "UI Flex":** Cinematic screen recordings of the "Admin Command Center", showing 1-click payouts.

### 3. Meta Ads (Targeting)
- **Interests:** Premier League, Fantasy Premier League, SportPesa, Betika.
- **Ad Creative:** "Tired of your mini-league admins delaying payouts? FantasyChama handles it instantly."
- **Geotargeting:** Nairobi, Mombasa, Nakuru (urban hubs with high smartphone/M-Pesa penetration).

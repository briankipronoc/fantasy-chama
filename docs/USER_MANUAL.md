# Fantasy Chama — App User Manual 📗

Welcome to the definitive guide for operating Fantasy Chama, the ultimate automated pot and verification engine for your FPL mini-leagues. Here is a breakdown of how our elite ecosystem works.

---

## 🧍 For Members: Playing & Paying

### 1. The Wallet System (Credits & Allowances)
Gone are the days of manually sending money every single week. Fantasy Chama operates on an advanced **Wallet/Credit** system:
*   Instead of paying weekly, you deposit a **lump sum** via M-Pesa STK push. 
*   Your Member Dashboard displays your total pooled `KES` balance under the **"Gameweek Status"** box.
*   The system continually monitors your balance. Your dashboard will show you exactly how many Gameweeks you are covered for (e.g., `Credits: 4 Gameweeks`).
*   As each gameweek resolves, the system securely deducts the exact stake from your Wallet. You do nothing!

### 2. The Verification Status
The app uses Action Banners to keep your league healthy:
*   **✅ Verified & Active (Green Zone)**: Your wallet balance covers the upcoming Gameweek. You are eligible to win the Pot!
*   **⚠️ Action Required (Red Zone)**: Your wallet is empty or negative. You must top-up before the FPL deadline, or your points will not count toward the Payout. If you remain unpaid for 2 consecutive gameweeks, the system disqualifies you from the vault.

### 3. Missing Payments & Disputes
Did you pay via M-Pesa Paybill directly without using the STK prompt, or did the network timeout? No problem.
*   On your dashboard, underneath the Pay button, click **Dispute payment →**.
*   Type in the exact **M-Pesa Receipt Code** (e.g., `SB49XY123Z`). 
*   The system will either instantly trace it with Safaricom, or securely flag the receipt to your Chairman for instant manual clearance.

### 4. Claiming the Crown
When you win the gameweek, you become the **Champion of the Week**.
*   The system will physically disburse the weekly pot automatically via M-Pesa B2C to your linked phone number.
*   Once you receive the text confirming the cash drop, a golden banner will appear on your dashboard. You **must click 'Confirm ✓'** to certify to the rest of the league that you received your rightful funds.

## 👑 For Chairmen & Admins: Governance

### 1. The Command Center & Master Ledger
The Chairman's Portal gives you absolute control over the financial timeline. The **Master Ledger** showcases real-time wallet balances, missing payments, and FPL IDs.
*   **Live Escrow Feed:** Located via the Ops feed. Watch your league autonomously update as members top up and bots calculate.

### 2. Assigning Co-Admins (Multi-Admin Control)
Relying on one person is a single point of failure. You can deputize your trusted members!
*   In the **Master Ledger**, locate the member you want to promote.
*   Click the **Make Admin** button inside their row.
*   That member can immediately log in via Phone Number and access the Command Center view.

### 3. Maker/Checker Withdrawals
To protect the vault's integrity, disbursements are locked behind a two-party system. 
*   When a gameweek ends, the **FPL Autopilot** algorithm selects the winner and submits a payment request to the Command Center.
*   **The Check:** One Admin/Chairman must review the pending payout and click **"Approve & Pay"**. Only then are the funds disbursed out of Safaricom's B2C engine.

### 4. Advanced Targeted Invites
You have full control over who joins the war room. In the **Invite Hub**:
*   You can set **Expiring Links**: Change the dropdown so the generated WhatsApp Link naturally destroys itself after 24 Hours.
*   You can assign **Targeted Phone Links**: In the "Target Phone" box, type the member's exact number. The generated link will mathematically lock out any other user from accessing it, preventing random players from stumbling into your financial league!
*   Members can also paste a 6-digit text code directly into the futuristic "OTP" input boxes when logging in.

### 5. Earn As You Lead (The Chairman's Cut)
Stop chasing friends for free. Managing a league takes effort, so Fantasy Chama automatically pays you to do it.
*   **The 3.5% Kickback**: Every time a Gameweek is resolved, the system calculates a 10% Escrow fee on the total pot. Out of that 10%, **3.5% goes directly to the Chairman** as a pure profit kickback. This is automatically deposited into your Wallet Balance as a `Deposit` transaction.
*   **The Auditor Split**: If you assigned a Co-Admin to help approve payouts, the system automatically shares the wealth to keep them incentivized! The 3.5% is split: **2.5% to the Chairman** and **1.0% to the Co-Admin**.
*   **Complete Transparency**: When the Gameweek ends, the automated WhatsApp receipt clearly displays the Escrow, Chairman, and Co-Admin cuts to all members, so there is zero hidden accounting.
